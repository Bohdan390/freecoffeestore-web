Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 May 2026

Free Coffee Store ("we", "us", "our") operates the Free Coffee Store mobile application (the "App"). This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Name — your full name, used to personalise your account.
  • Email address — used to create and secure your account, send verification and password-reset emails, and contact you about your redemptions.
  • Physical (shipping) address — collected only when you redeem coffee, used solely to ship your reward to you.

Information collected automatically:

  • User ID — a unique identifier we assign to your account.
  • Device ID — your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS), collected by our advertising SDK.
  • Usage data — product interaction, ad views and taps, and other analytics generated by the advertising SDK.
  • Diagnostics — crash logs and performance metrics used to keep the App stable.

2. How we use your information

  • To operate the App, including account creation, login, and credit balance management.
  • To fulfil coffee redemptions and ship them to your provided address.
  • To serve in-App advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • To measure performance, fix crashes, and improve the App.
  • To communicate with you about your account, security, and support requests.

3. Third-party services

We rely on the following providers, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and account hosting. supabase.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads) — in-App advertising and ad-related diagnostics. policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Sign-In — optional sign-in with a Google account. policies.google.com/privacy

4. Tracking and App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS we use Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. When you first launch the App, you will be asked whether you allow tracking. If you decline, advertising identifiers will not be shared with Google AdMob for personalised advertising, but you will still see non-personalised ads.

5. Data linked to you

The following data types are linked to your identity: name, email address, physical address, and user ID. Device ID, advertising data, product interaction, and diagnostics are collected by Google AdMob and are not linked to your identity by us.

6. Data retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account (Profile → Delete Account, or by emailing us), we delete your profile and redemption history from our database. Records that we are required to keep for tax, legal, or fraud-prevention reasons may be retained for the legally required period.

7. Your rights

You may, at any time:

  • Access or update your profile inside the App.
  • Delete your account inside the App (Profile → Delete Account).
  • Email us at info@freecoffeestore.com to request a copy of your data or its deletion.
  • Withdraw advertising tracking consent at any time from iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

8. Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA/UK). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. International users

Your information is stored on servers operated by Supabase and may be processed in countries outside your own. We take appropriate measures to ensure your data remains protected.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Material changes will also be communicated inside the App.

11. Contact

Questions or requests? Email info@freecoffeestore.com.

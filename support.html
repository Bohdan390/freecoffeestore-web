Frequently asked questions

How do I earn credits?

Open the app and you'll see several ways to earn:

  • Watch a video ad — quick credits, any time.
  • Complete a short survey — share your opinion, earn more per task.
  • Daily check-in — open the app every day for a free bonus.
  • Refer a friend — both of you get a bonus when they sign up.

How do I redeem my credits for coffee?

Once you have enough credits, tap Redeem on the home screen, enter your shipping address, and confirm. Your order will move through these stages: Pending → Confirmed → Shipped → Delivered.

How long does shipping take?

Most orders ship within 3–5 business days. Delivery time depends on your location. If a tracking number is available, it will appear on the Redemption History screen.

I can't sign in. What do I do?

Use the Forgot Password link on the login screen to reset your password by email. If you signed up with Google, tap Continue with Google instead. If you're still stuck, email us at info@freecoffeestore.com.

I didn't receive my email verification.

Check your spam folder first. You can request a new verification email from the app's login screen. If it still doesn't arrive, contact support.

Can I cancel a redemption?

Yes — you can cancel any order that is still in Pending status. Open Redemption History, find the order, and tap Cancel. Your credits will be refunded immediately. Orders that have been confirmed or shipped can no longer be cancelled.

How do I update my shipping address?

The shipping address is entered fresh for every redemption, so you can change it on each order. There is no saved default address.

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, go to Profile, and tap Delete Account. This permanently removes your profile, credits, and redemption history. Alternatively, email info@freecoffeestore.com with the subject "Delete my account" and we will remove your data within 7 days.

Why am I seeing ads?

Ads are how we fund the free coffee. Watching them is also the fastest way to earn credits. You can choose to allow or decline ad tracking when iOS asks — either way, you'll still see ads, but tracking-off ads are non-personalised.

Is the app really free?

Yes. No fees, no subscriptions, no credit card. You earn credits through normal in-app activity and redeem them for real coffee that we ship to you at no cost.

Still need help?

Email info@freecoffeestore.com with as much detail as possible (account email, order ID if relevant, screenshots) and we'll get back to you.
